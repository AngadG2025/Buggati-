A Legacy of Excellence

The Bugatti Story

Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, Bugatti has been synonymous with automotive excellence for over a century. The French luxury brand has continuously pushed the boundaries of performance, design, and engineering.

Ettore Bugatti's philosophy was simple yet profound: "Nothing is too beautiful, nothing is too expensive." This mantra continues to guide the brand today, creating some of the most exclusive and powerful supercars in the world.

From the iconic Type 35 Grand Prix racer to the revolutionary Veyron and the record-breaking Chiron, Bugatti has consistently demonstrated that automotive art and technical perfection can coexist.
